Vladimir Putin is a criminal, a dictator and a murderer who should be judged by a magistrate's court. Will you continue to engage in musical activism and do you still have your radical leftist views?

I still hold leftist views, but I'm disappointed in music as a tool of struggle. Because any idea can be turned into a business - it devalues ​​any activism when your track depends on algorithms on Spotify. I'd rather make money for myself, and people will somehow figure it out without my music. Most likely, my new music will be more personal than politicized.
